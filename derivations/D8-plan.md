# D8 — Swampland × NMC cross-constraint (plan)

## Question
If χ (A4) and the KK tower (A5) are both bulk modes of the same dark sector,
they share a common EFT cutoff Λ. Does combining the Swampland Distance
Conjecture / Dark-Dimension relation
$$\Lambda \sim M_P (H/M_P)^{c'}$$
with the Cassini PPN bound on ξ_χ (D7) sharpen |ξ_χ| beyond 2.4×10⁻²?

## Strategy
1. Encode Λ(c', H) from the user's definition.
2. Impose the EFT self-consistency condition on the operator
   (ξ_χ/2) R χ²: require $\xi_\chi \chi_0^2 \le \Lambda^2$ (modification of
   the effective Planck mass capped by the cutoff squared).
3. Translate into a bound on χ₀/M_P as a function of (c', ξ_χ).
4. Feed into Cassini PPN `|ξ_χ| (χ₀/M_P) ≲ 2.4×10⁻³`.
5. Sweep (c', ξ_χ), plot allowed overlap.
6. Check limits: c'→0 must recover D7 Cassini-only; ξ_χ→0 must recover
   minimal-coupling SDC (trivial).
7. Honest verdict: does it tighten |ξ_χ| by >30%? If yes, draft §3.6.

## Non-goals
- No new paper claim unless the tightening is robust.
- No modification of D1–D7 or eci.tex body.
