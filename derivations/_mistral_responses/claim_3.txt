Let's break down the problem step-by-step to find the constraint on the non-minimal coupling \(\xi\) for the scalar field \(\chi\) in the Dark Dimension scenario.

---

### **Given:**
1. **Species-scale cutoff:**
   \[
   \Lambda_{\text{species}}(H) \approx M_P \left( \frac{H}{M_P} \right)^{c'}, \quad c' = \frac{1}{6}
   \]
2. **Non-minimal coupling term:**
   \[
   \frac{1}{2} \xi R \chi^2
   \]
   This modifies the effective Planck mass as:
   \[
   M_P^2 \to M_P^2 + \xi \chi^2
   \]
   The shift in the Planck mass is:
   \[
   \delta M_P^2 = \xi \chi^2
   \]
3. **Heuristic condition:**
   \[
   \delta M_P^2 \leq \Lambda_{\text{species}}^2
   \]
4. **Values today (\(H = H_0\)):**
   - \(\chi_0 = \frac{M_P}{10}\)
   - \(H_0 \approx 2.2 \times 10^{-33} \, \text{eV}\) (present Hubble parameter)
   - \(M_P \approx 2.4 \times 10^{27} \, \text{eV}\) (reduced Planck mass)

---

### **Step 1: Compute \(\Lambda_{\text{species}}(H_0)\)**
Using the given formula:
\[
\Lambda_{\text{species}}(H_0) = M_P \left( \frac{H_0}{M_P} \right)^{1/6}
\]
Substitute \(H_0\) and \(M_P\):
\[
\frac{H_0}{M_P} = \frac{2.2 \times 10^{-33}}{2.4 \times 10^{27}} \approx 9.17 \times 10^{-61}
\]
Now compute the exponent:
\[
\left( 9.17 \times 10^{-61} \right)^{1/6} \approx (9.17)^{1/6} \times 10^{-61/6} \approx 1.45 \times 10^{-10.17} \approx 1.45 \times 10^{-10}
\]
Thus:
\[
\Lambda_{\text{species}}(H_0) \approx 2.4 \times 10^{27} \times 1.45 \times 10^{-10} \approx 3.5 \times 10^{17} \, \text{eV}
\]
Square this to get \(\Lambda_{\text{species}}^2\):
\[
\Lambda_{\text{species}}^2 \approx (3.5 \times 10^{17})^2 \approx 1.2 \times 10^{35} \, \text{eV}^2
\]

---

### **Step 2: Apply the heuristic condition**
The condition is:
\[
\delta M_P^2 = \xi \chi_0^2 \leq \Lambda_{\text{species}}^2
\]
Substitute \(\chi_0 = \frac{M_P}{10}\):
\[
\xi \left( \frac{M_P}{10} \right)^2 \leq \Lambda_{\text{species}}^2
\]
\[
\xi \frac{M_P^2}{100} \leq 1.2 \times 10^{35} \, \text{eV}^2
\]
Substitute \(M_P^2 = (2.4 \times 10^{27})^2 \approx 5.8 \times 10^{54} \, \text{eV}^2\):
\[
\xi \frac{5.8 \times 10^{54}}{100} \leq 1.2 \times 10^{35}
\]
\[
\xi \times 5.8 \times 10^{52} \leq 1.2 \times 10^{35}
\]
Solve for \(\xi\):
\[
\xi \leq \frac{1.2 \times 10^{35}}{5.8 \times 10^{52}} \approx 2.1 \times 10^{-18}
\]

---

### **Final Result:**
The constraint on the non-minimal coupling is:
\[
|\xi| \leq 2.1 \times 10^{-18}
\]

This is an extremely small bound, reflecting the tight EFT constraint in the Dark Dimension scenario.
