## Strategic conclusion: where ECI's path sits

1. **Math-anchor (CM by Q(i), Damerell 4.5.b.a, LMFDB 16.5.c.a)** is unique — no TOP-5 model derives τ=i from period theory or Hecke arithmetic; they all *assume* or *empirically fit* τ near i. ECI provides a **number-theoretic reason**.
2. **SU(5) GUT closure (G1.12.B M1–M5 PASS)** is genuinely additional — only Du-Wang's flipped-SU(5) intersects, and uses A_4 not S'_4.
3. **Cosmological wing (A11 Bianchi, A20 BIX-LISA)** is orthogonal to all 5 BSM flavour models; only KW's dS-trap touches cosmo at a different layer.
4. **Parameter economy** is ECI's WORST suit: A17 gives only 2 CKM numerological hits with empirical coefficients (9/4, 1) — Feruglio (N=7) and NPP20 (N=4–6) are far more economical for leptons; LYD20 (N=9–10) is comparable for joint Q+L but with full fit machinery ECI lacks.
5. **W1 τ-attractor for full PMNS REFUTED** (A16: sin²θ_13 24.5σ off; reconstruction: χ²(τ=i exact)=1240 vs χ²(LYD-style τ)=690 → 1.8× penalty when integrated over 5 free Yukawas).

## Bottom-line: REPLACE with two-tau or KEEP as math-anchor?

**KEEP single-τ at i as math-anchor; do NOT migrate to two-tau.**

- (i) **Du-Wang's own data** (single-τ IX' χ²=1.558 vs dual-τ χ²=95) show two-modulus generalisation **degrades fit by ~60×**.
- (ii) ECI's value-add is the **CM-anchor at K=Q(i)** (cross-K-tested at A14, A17, A24); migrating to two τ's destroys K=Q(i)-uniqueness.
- (iii) Path forward: **REFORMULATE A14 littlest-modular-seesaw as v7.4 §3 anchor** + **GRAFT LYD20's unified Q+L scaffold as v7.4 §4** with τ pinned at i.

**Net v7.4 → v7.5 = CM-anchored attractor + LYD20-scaffold + Kähler-quark-hierarchy + KW-dS-backstop**, NOT pure single-τ-everywhere and NOT two-τ.
