## Core result

For every unitary 2D CFT carrying a diagonal (A-series) modular-invariant partition function with central charge c > 0, the analog-Hawking saturation ratio

    ρ := (2π)⁻² ∫₀^∞ S_BW(u) du

satisfies ρ = c/12 exactly, where S_BW(u) is the Bisognano–Wichmann single-mode von Neumann entropy at dimensionless modular frequency u.

## Why this belongs in LMP

- The result is exact (not an approximation), connects three established pillars of mathematical physics (Cardy formula, Bisognano–Wichmann theorem, Riemann zeta function), and is new in the sense stated precisely in §1.
