Let S'_4 be the metaplectic double cover of the modular symmetry group S_4, introduced by Novichkov, Penedo, and Petcov (arXiv:2006.03058). We identify two of its hatted weight-5 multiplets with classical newforms in the LMFDB.

The hatted triplet 3-hat,2(5) coincides with the unique CM newform 4.5.b.a (level 4, weight 5, nebentypus chi_4, CM by Q(i), root number +1): the identification is established by four independent methods, including the Groessencharacter formula a(p) = 2 Re((a + bi)^4) for p = a^2 + b^2, verified at five primes p in {5, 13, 17, 29, 37} beyond the Sturm bound.

The hatted doublet 2-hat(5) coincides with 16.5.c.a (level 16, weight 5, coefficient field Q(sqrt(-3)), not CM), verified at 14 primes up to p = 113.

As a corollary, the sub-algebra H_1 = {T(p) : p == 1 (mod 4)} acts diagonally on each multiplet, while T(p) for p == 3 (mod 4) is obstructed (10/10 primes tested). This obstruction is an immediate consequence of the chi_4-nebentypus.

The first identification transfers the completed L-function and the Damerell-Chowla-Selberg algebraic special values (expressible via Gamma(1/4)) to the metaplectic setting.
