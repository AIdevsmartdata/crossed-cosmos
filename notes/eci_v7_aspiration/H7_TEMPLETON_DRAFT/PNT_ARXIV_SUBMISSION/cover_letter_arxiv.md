# Optional cover note / endorsement-request blurb

**Use case:** if requesting a math.NT endorsement, you may attach this short note
to your endorsement request email. arXiv itself does not require a cover letter
for submission — only a journal cover letter is required (already prepared at
`/root/crossed-cosmos/notes/eci_v7_aspiration/PNT/cover_letter_blms.md` for BLMS).

Dear Professor [NAME],

I am writing to request your endorsement for an arXiv submission to the
math.NT category. I am an independent researcher based in Tarbes, France, and
the paper I wish to submit is titled

  "Two LMFDB identifications for hatted weight-5 multiplets of the
   metaplectic cover S'_4."

The paper (11 pages, amsart class, target journal Bulletin of the London
Mathematical Society) establishes two identifications between hatted
weight-5 multiplets of the metaplectic cover S'_4 — recently introduced by
Novichkov, Penedo, and Petcov (Nucl. Phys. B 963, 2021; arXiv:2006.03058) — and
classical newforms tabulated in the LMFDB:

  (1) the hatted triplet 3-hat,2(5) coincides with the unique CM newform
      4.5.b.a (level 4, weight 5, nebentypus chi_4, CM by Q(i), root number +1);
  (2) the hatted doublet 2-hat(5) coincides with 16.5.c.a (level 16, weight 5,
      coefficient field Q(sqrt(-3)), not CM).

The first identification is verified at five primes beyond the Sturm bound
B = 3 via four independent methods, including the Groessencharacter formula
a(p) = 2 Re((a + bi)^4); the second is verified at 14 primes up to p = 113,
well beyond B = 10. As a corollary, the Hecke sub-algebra generated by T(p)
for p == 1 (mod 4) acts diagonally on each multiplet, while T(p) for
p == 3 (mod 4) is obstructed.

The paper opens a natural classification programme — extending these identi-
fications to all odd-weight hatted multiplets and to other metaplectic covers
(A'_4, A'_5, ...) — that bridges the modular flavour symmetry literature and
the LMFDB. All LMFDB labels and arXiv references were live-verified on
2026-05-05; the full audit chain (sympy verification scripts, eigenvalue logs,
LMFDB live-fetch records) is archived at Zenodo (concept doi:10.5281/zenodo.19686398;
current record doi:10.5281/zenodo.20034969).

I would be very grateful if you could consider endorsing this submission.
I am happy to send the manuscript and the verification code to you in advance
of the formal arXiv endorsement, should that be helpful.
