## §S1 — Bianchi IX × type-II_∞ Modular Shadow [VIABLE-NEW-PISTE, priority HIGH]

### The construction (sketch)

Let $\mathcal{M}_{\rm BIX}$ denote the moduli of Bianchi IX vacuum spacetimes
(SO(3)-symmetric, 3 scale factors). On the kasner-billiard reduction
(Misner variables), Manin-Marcolli 2015 (arXiv:1504.04005) prove a
**hard correspondence**:

$$
\text{Mixmaster BKL bounce sequence} \quad \longleftrightarrow \quad
\text{geodesic flow on } X(2) = \Gamma(2)\backslash\mathbb{H}
$$

via two-sided continued-fraction shift $\sigma$. The shift $\sigma$ is the
classical Gauss map on $[0,1]$, and on $X(2)$ it is the geodesic flow
restricted to imaginary axis.

**Step A (verified):** $\sigma$ admits a unique invariant measure of maximal entropy
$\mu_{\rm Gauss}$ (Gauss measure on $[0,1]$). This is the **AWCH measure** in our
ECI Bianchi IX Lemma A.1 setup, identified with Marcolli's Gauss-shift measure.

**Step B (proposed conjecture, NEW):** The Hilbert space
$\mathcal{H}_{\rm BIX} = L^2(X(2), \mu_{\rm Gauss})$ supports a faithful normal
representation $\pi: \mathcal{N} \to B(\mathcal{H}_{\rm BIX})$ of a type-III_1
factor $\mathcal{N}$ generated by gauge-invariant local field operators on
the Bianchi IX vacuum. The modular automorphism $\Delta^{it}_{\rm Gauss}$
on $\mathcal{N}$ is implemented by the geodesic-flow shift $\sigma^t$.

**Step C (proposed conjecture, NEW):** The crossed product
$\mathcal{N} \rtimes_\sigma \mathbb{R}$ is a type-II_∞ factor with
semifinite trace $\tau$, providing the **Bianchi IX Modular Shadow algebra**
$\mathcal{M}_{\rm BIX}^{\rm II_\infty}$. Following Witten 2022 (arXiv:2112.12828)
and Speranza 2025 (arXiv:2504.07630), this is the natural seat of the observer's
gravitationally-dressed algebra.

**Step D (target conjecture, HEAVY [TBD: prove]):** The Krylov-complexity
saturation $K(t)/t \le 2\pi/\beta$ on $\mathcal{M}_{\rm BIX}^{\rm II_\infty}$
follows from the MSS bound applied to the Manin-Marcolli geodesic flow at
$\beta = 2\pi/\lambda_{\rm BKL}$ where $\lambda_{\rm BKL} \in [0, 1]$ is the
positive Kolmogorov-Sinai entropy of the BKL Kasner map. This is a
**falsifiable** Modular-Shadow extension to anisotropic gravity.

equivalent up to invertible 2-cell, so we package into stable
