## P6-A1: SUPPORTER (potentially valuable for ECI Paper 2)

**ID:** 2604.13854v1
**DATE:** 2026-04-15
**AUTHORS:** Kâzım Büyükboduk, Peter Neamti
**TITLE:** A proof of p-adic Gross–Zagier theorem via BDP formula
**ABSTRACT (verified):** New proof of p-adic Gross–Zagier formula for the p-adic L-function of the base change of a normalized cuspidal eigen-newform f of weight k ≥ 2 to imaginary quadratic fields. Handles both ordinary and non-ordinary cases including new scenarios k > 2. Uses wall-crossing strategy via BDP formula and Beilinson-Flach elements.
**CLASSIFICATION: SUPPORTER**
**ECI RELEVANCE:** Weight k ≥ 2 cover weight k=5 (ECI's CM newform 4.5.b.a). Base change to imaginary quadratic fields is exactly the K=Q(i) extension ECI uses. The p-adic Gross-Zagier formula connects p-adic L-functions to height pairings of CM points — directly adjacent to the Damerell ladder L(f,m)·π^{4-m}/Ω_K^4 that ECI's H7' uses. BDP formula = Bertolini-Darmon-Prasanna formula (one of the three A76 rescue channels: H7-C Bertolini-Darmon-Prasanna 2013 anti-cyclotomic p-adic alternative). A 2026 paper proving p-adic GZ via BDP for k > 2 ordinary and non-ordinary cases is directly relevant to ECI's Adelic Katz paper-2 (MEDIUM priority item from D3.3).
**STRATEGIC NOTE:** This paper should be cited in ECI's math.NT paper-2 (Katz-type p-adic L-functions for 4.5.b.a). The non-ordinary case at k=5 may be relevant since 4.5.b.a is CM and has specific ordinary/non-ordinary properties at p=2.
**NOTE:** Live-verified at https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13854. Authors: Kâzım Büyükboduk, Peter Neamti.

## P6-A2: ADJACENT

**ID:** 2604.20662v1
**DATE:** 2026-04-22
**AUTHORS:** Jennifer S. Balakrishnan, Francesca Bianchi, Netan Dogra
**TITLE:** p-adic elliptic polylogarithms and cubic Chabauty
**ABSTRACT (verified):** Explicit formulas for Chabauty-Coleman-Kim sets using p-adic elliptic polylogarithms. Extends quadratic Chabauty to cubic depth. Gives integral points on hyperelliptic curves through zeros of polynomial in p-adic elliptic polylogarithms. Verifies instances of Kim's conjecture. Uses special values of p-adic L-functions.
**CLASSIFICATION: ADJACENT**
**ECI RELEVANCE:** Kim's method + p-adic elliptic polylogarithms — this is in the same broad area as ECI's adelic p-adic L-function work (via Katz, Andreatta-Iovita). However, cubic Chabauty focuses on rational/integral points on curves, not on special values of CM L-functions per se. ADJACENT — shares technical vocabulary (p-adic L-functions, Chabauty) but different object of study (integral points vs. CM special values).
**NOTE:** Live-verified at https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20662.
