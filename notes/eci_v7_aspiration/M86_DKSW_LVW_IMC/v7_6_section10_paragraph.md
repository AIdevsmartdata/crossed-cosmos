# v7.6 §10 — DKSW23 × LVW paragraph (80 words trimmed)

> Two recent programmes advance the tools for Conjecture R-2.1. Dasgupta-Kakde-Silliman-Wang (arXiv:2310.16399) prove Brumer-Stark over Z, closing p=2 ramified with integral precision via group-ring Hilbert Eisenstein and Ribet's method. Longo-Vigni-Wang (arXiv:2501.03673) extend BDP Heegner cycles to algebraic Hecke characters of infinity type (1+ℓ,-ℓ), yielding an explicit Rubin formula outside interpolation range. Neither covers (K=Q(i), p=2 ramified, k=5 odd) directly; adapting DKSW23 to imaginary quadratic base fields and extending LVW to even discriminant and odd weight remains open (cf. M44.1(b), M75 F-1, M57). [TBD: prove]
