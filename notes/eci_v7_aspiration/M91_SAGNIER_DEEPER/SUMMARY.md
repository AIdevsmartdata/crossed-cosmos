## Primary obstruction: REPRESENTATION-THEORETIC

Sagnier's framework: Hilbert space L²(A_K^× / K^× × K_∞^1, d^×x) where K_∞^1 = S^1 for K=Q(i). Characters factoring through compact S^1 = finite-order angular chars χ_n(e^{iθ}) = e^{inθ} are L².

Algebraic weight chars ψ_{k-1}: z ↦ (z/|z|)^{k-1} · |z|^{k-1} contain non-compact factor |z|^{k-1} from C^× / S^1 ≅ ℝ_{>0}^×. NOT L² without twisting by |·|^{-(k-1)/2}, which restructures the entire spectral construction.

**Secondary obstruction: gamma factor.** L(s, ψ_{k-1}) has Γ_C(s + (k-1)/2) instead of Γ_C(s) → critical line shifts per weight k. Site rebuild per k OR family of sites needed.
