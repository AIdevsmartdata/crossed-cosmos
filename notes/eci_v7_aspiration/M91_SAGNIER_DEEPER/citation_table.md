## Extension status taxonomy

- **RIGID**: paper explicitly restricts to a single L-function (ζ only) and proves this is necessary
- **ζ only**: restricted to Riemann zeta; no attempt to extend
- **ζ_K only**: restricted to Dedekind zeta of K; no non-trivial characters
- **finite-order only**: covers characters factoring through the compact group K_∞^1 = S^1; angular characters
- **special values**: covers algebraic Hecke characters but via special values (not spectral interpretation of zeros)
- **Q only / Q scaling**: works over Q, not imag. quadratic fields
