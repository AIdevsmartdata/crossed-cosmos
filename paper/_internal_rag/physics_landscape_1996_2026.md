# physics_landscape_1996_2026.md
# 30-Year Map of Fundamental-Physics Models (1996–2026)
# Compiled 2026-04-22 — structured survey, not advocacy

**Legend — Status codes**
| Code | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| ACTIVE | Research ongoing, not empirically refuted, publications post-2023 |
| FADING | Technically alive but losing community traction or severely constrained |
| REFUTED | Empirically or mathematically ruled out at community-accepted confidence |
| DORMANT | Not refuted but minimal recent activity |
| CONTESTED | Disputed derivation, split community verdict |

Uncertainty flags: ⚠ = year/author uncertain from training knowledge; verify before citing.

## 1. Quantum Gravity & Space-Time Emergence

| Model | Proposer + year | Core claim | Status 2026 | Decisive event |
|-------|----------------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **String / M-theory** | Green-Schwarz-Witten 1984; M-theory: Witten 1995 | Ten/eleven-dimensional superstring unification; gravity + gauge in single framework | ACTIVE | No LHC SUSY signal; landscape ∼10^500 vacua remain; programme alive at formal level |
| **RS1 / RS2 braneworlds** | Randall-Sundrum 1999 | Two branes (RS1) or single brane with infinite extra dim (RS2) generate hierarchy or DGP gravity | FADING | LHC Run 1–3: no KK gravitons, no missing-energy signal at ∼TeV; RS1 natural hierarchy fine-tuned |
| **KKLT de Sitter vacua** | Kachru-Kallosh-Linde-Trivedi 2003 | Uplift of supersymmetric AdS vacua to metastable dS via anti-D3 branes | CONTESTED | Bena-Polchinski-Roiban 2019 + follow-ups contest stability; refined dS conjecture (Obied et al. 2018) casts doubt; debate unresolved 2026 |
| **DBI inflation** | Silverstein-Tong 2004 | Brane inflation with Dirac-Born-Infeld kinetic term; equilateral non-Gaussianity | FADING | Planck 2018 tight f_NL equil < 26 at 2σ; DBI parameter space severely constrained |
| **Loop Quantum Gravity (LQG)** | Rovelli-Smolin 1988; spin-network basis 1995 | Quantise geometry via SU(2) connection; discrete spectra of area/volume operators | ACTIVE | Black hole entropy derivation, Immirzi parameter fixed; no direct empirical test yet |
| **Loop Quantum Cosmology (LQC)** | Bojowald 2001; Ashtekar-Singh 2011 | LQG applied to FLRW minisuperspace; big-bounce replaces singularity | ACTIVE | Self-consistent bounce dynamics; modified dispersion relations; quantum gravity corrections to inflation studied |
| **Causal Dynamical Triangulations (CDT)** | Ambjorn-Jurkiewicz-Loll 1998 | Path-integral over Lorentzian piecewise-linear geometries; 4D de Sitter phase emerges | ACTIVE | 4D semiclassical geometry recovered numerically; spectral dimension running observed; no quantum field coupling yet |
| **Asymptotic Safety** | Weinberg 1979 (idea); Reuter 1998 (concrete flow); Eichhorn 2018+ | Non-perturbative UV fixed point for gravity; finite-parameter renormalisation | ACTIVE | Functional RG (Wetterich eq.) studies confirm candidate fixed point; matter coupling constraints published 2019–2025 |
| **Group Field Theory (GFT)** | Oriti 2005+; Krajewski 2011 | QFT on group manifold; Feynman diagrams = simplicial quantum gravity amplitudes; condensate cosmology | ACTIVE | Cosmological condensate phase reproduces Friedmann dynamics at mean-field; Oriti-Sindoni-Wilson-Ewing 2016+ |
| **Spin Foam / EPRL amplitudes** | Engle-Pereira-Rovelli-Livine 2008; Vidotto 2011+ | Lorentzian spinfoam amplitudes for LQG transition amplitudes; EPRL model | ACTIVE | Numerical vertex calculations ongoing; relation to CDT explored; Hellmann-Kaminski 2013+ |
| **Shape Dynamics** | Barbour-O'Murchadha 2002; Gryb-Thébault-Koslowski 2012+ | Reframe GR in terms of conformal geometry with global-simultaneity gauge; York time | ACTIVE (niche) | Classically equivalent to GR in many sectors; quantum version under study; small community |
| **Causal Set Theory** | Sorkin 1991+; Dowker 2005+ | Spacetime = locally finite partial order; Lorentz invariance exact at discrete level | ACTIVE | Causal set scalar field, sum-over-orders studied; Benincasa-Dowker d'Alembertian 2010 |
| **Noncommutative Geometry SM** | Connes 1994; Chamseddine-Connes-Marcolli 2007 | Standard Model + Higgs as spectral triple on product M₄ × F; Higgs mass prediction | FADING | 2011 prediction M_H ≈ 170 GeV falsified by LHC (M_H = 125 GeV); post-hoc modifications patch but weaken predictivity |
| **Crossed-product type-II (CLPW)** | Chandrasekaran-Longo-Penington-Witten 2023; De Vuyst-Eccles-Höhn-Kirklin 2025 | Observer-dependent type-II von Neumann algebra for static dS / SdS; generalised entropy as functor | ACTIVE | Established theorem for dS static patch; DEHK 2025 extends to SdS Killing horizons; used in ECI v6 (our work) |
| **ER = EPR / traversable wormholes** | Maldacena-Susskind 2013; Gao-Jafferis-Wall 2017 | Geometric ER bridge dual to quantum EPR entanglement; traversable wormholes via double-trace deformation | ACTIVE (conjectural) | CLPW 2023 provides algebraic container; Susskind-Stanford complexity=volume remains CONTESTED (our toy TFD r=0.91, not proportional) |
| **AdS/CFT + islands + Page curve** | Penington 2019; Almheiri-Mahajan-Maldacena-Zhao 2019 | Island formula for entanglement entropy reproduces Page curve; resolves information paradox in JT gravity | ACTIVE | Sunholds for JT gravity, 2D dilaton models; extension to realistic 4D black holes remains open; Penington-Almheiri 2019 seminal |
| **Amplituhedron / positive geometry** | Arkani-Hamed-Trnka 2013+ | S-matrix of N=4 SYM as volume of polytope in Grassmannian; no locality/unitarity as inputs | ACTIVE | Extended to cosmological polytopes (Arkani-Hamed-Benincasa-Postnikov 2017); superstring amplitudes; no direct falsifiable phenomenology yet |
| **Celestial holography** | Strominger et al. 2016–2020+ | Soft theorems = Ward identities of asymptotic symmetries (BMS, w∞); hologram on celestial sphere | ACTIVE | Celestal OPE, Carrollian CFT formulation 2021–2025; Himwich-Kapec-O'Connell-Strominger; no empirical test |
| **Tensor networks / MERA** | Vidal 2007; Pastawski-Yoshida-Harlow-Preskill 2015 | Holographic tensor networks (MERA, HaPPY) as toy AdS/CFT; error-correcting codes | ACTIVE | Rigorous in toy models; PFC / uberholography extensions; no quantum gravity content beyond AdS/CFT |

## 2. Gravity Modifications

| Model | Proposer + year | Core claim | Status 2026 | Decisive event |
|-------|----------------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **f(R) gravity** | Various; Starobinsky 1980 (R²); Carroll et al. 2004 revival | Replace R by f(R) in Einstein-Hilbert; extra scalar DOF via conformal frame | ACTIVE | R² = Starobinsky inflation best-fit Planck; late-time f(R) DE models constrained by IST/growth; viable narrow window |
| **Brans-Dicke / scalar-tensor** | Brans-Dicke 1961; Bergmann-Wagoner 1968 | Scalar field Φ mediates gravity; ω parameter; GR limit ω→∞ | FADING | Cassini: ω > 40 000; post-Newtonian tests kill most BD DE models; conformal coupling special case survives (see NMC quintessence) |
| **Horndeski / DHOST** | Horndeski 1974; Gleyzes et al. 2015 (DHOST); Langlois-Noui 2016 | Most general 4D scalar-tensor theory with 2nd-order EOM; extended beyond Horndeski | FADING (severe) | GW170817+GRB170817A: c_T = c at 10^{-15}; kills most Galileon/Horndeski dark-energy subclasses; rump survives |
| **Massive gravity (dRGT)** | de Rham-Gabadadze-Tolley 2011 | Ghost-free massive graviton; Fierz-Pauli at linear order but nonlinear Boulware-Deser ghost-free | ACTIVE (constrained) | m_g < 1.27×10^{-22} eV from GW170817; solar-system Vainshtein screening required; dark-energy applications fading |
| **Bimetric gravity (Hassan-Rosen)** | Hassan-Rosen 2012 | Two dynamical metrics coupled via interaction potential; one massless + one massive graviton | ACTIVE | Bigravity cosmology viable in some branches; GW speed constraint applies to massive sector |
| **MOND + TeVeS** | Milgrom 1983 (MOND); Bekenstein 2004 (TeVeS) | Modified inertia/gravity below a₀ ≈ 1.2×10^{-10} m/s²; TeVeS as relativistic completion | CONTESTED | TeVeS: Bullet Cluster requires DM (2006); gravitational lensing without DM fails; GW170817 kills original TeVeS (c_T ≠ c). Revised relativistic completions (Skordis-Złośnik 2021) partially survive |
| **MOG (Moffat)** | Moffat 2006 | Modified gravity with variable G; vector field mediates attraction | FADING | Cluster lensing and CMB fit poor vs ΛCDM; small community |
| **Hořava-Lifshitz gravity** | Hořava 2009 | Power-counting renormalisable gravity with anisotropic scaling; breaks Lorentz invariance in UV | ACTIVE (theoretical) | IR limit to GR requires fine-tuning (strong coupling problem); quantum gravity programme continues; no phenomenology dominant |
| **Conformal gravity (Mannheim)** | Mannheim-Kazanas 1989; Mannheim 2012+ | Weyl-squared action; 4th-order EOM; claimed galactic rotation curves | REFUTED (contested) | Gravitational lensing incompatible; ghost problem; see Flanagan 2006; largely rejected by community |
| **Unimodular gravity** | Buchmuller-Dragon 1988; Weinberg 1989 | Restrict √-g = 1; Λ becomes integration constant, not vacuum energy; trace-free Einstein eqs | ACTIVE (niche) | No new predictions vs GR; cosmological constant problem recast but not solved; active small programme |
| **Mimetic gravity** | Chamseddine-Mukhanov 2013 | Isolate longitudinal scalar DOF of metric by conformal reparametrisation; mimics DM from geometry | ACTIVE (niche) | Ghost-free versions constructed; loop corrections studied; no empirical test distinct from DM |
| **Einstein-aether / Lorentz violation** | Jacobson-Mattingly 2001+ | Vector field defines preferred frame; Lorentz-violating gravity with aether coupling | FADING | GW170817: c_T = c at 10^{-15} kills aether-tensor parameter range; khronometric model residual |
| **Palatini extensions** | Various | Metric-affine or Palatini variational principle; f(R) Palatini different from metric f(R) | ACTIVE | Palatini f(R) avoids Ostrogradski ghosts; inflationary applications active (Rasanen 2018+) |
| **Einstein-Gauss-Bonnet in 4D (Glavan-Lin)** | Glavan-Lin 2020 | Regularised 4D EGB gravity; claimed finite gravitational coupling from topological term | CONTESTED → LARGELY REFUTED | Gurses-Heydari-Sisman-Tekin 2020: procedure ill-defined; Arrechea-Delhom-Jimenez 2020: singular limit; consensus: 4D EGB as written is not a consistent theory |

## 3. Dark Sector Alternatives

| Model | Proposer + year | Core claim | Status 2026 | Decisive event |
|-------|----------------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Cold Dark Matter (CDM)** | Davis et al. 1985 (ΛCDM formed 1990s) | Massive non-relativistic weakly-interacting particles; WIMP paradigm | ACTIVE (standard) | CMB, LSS, BAO precise fits; WIMP direct detection null (LZ 2022, XENONnT 2023) shifts preferred models |
| **Warm Dark Matter (WDM)** | Bond-Efstathiou-Silk 1980+; Dodelson-Widrow 1994 | keV-scale DM; free-streaming cuts small-scale power | ACTIVE | Lyman-α forest constraints m_WDM > 5.3 keV (2021+); sterile neutrino parameter space constrained |
| **Fuzzy DM / ultralight axions** | Hu-Sawicki-Zaldarriaga 2000; Hui-Ostriker-Tremaine-Witten 2017 | m ∼ 10^{-22} eV axion; quantum pressure suppresses sub-kpc structure | ACTIVE (contested) | Lyman-α: m > 2×10^{-21} eV (Irsic 2017); tension with soliton core predictions; Milky Way subhalo counts | 
| **Primordial Black Hole DM (PBH)** | Hawking 1971; Carr 1975; Clesse-Garcia-Bellido 2017 revival | PBHs formed in radiation era; asteroid-mass window ∼10^{17}–10^{22} g partially open | ACTIVE | LIGO O3 mass function; microlensing (Subaru HSC, EROS-2) close asteroid window; sub-solar window 10^{17}–10^{20} g remains open |
| **Superfluid DM** | Berezhiani-Khoury 2015 | DM is superfluid on galaxy scales; MOND as phonon-mediated force | ACTIVE (niche) | Reproduces MOND-like fits; no CMB/cluster prediction distinct from CDM at large scale; no direct test |
| **Emergent DM (Verlinde 2017)** | Verlinde 2017 | Apparent DM from elastic response of entanglement entropy; dark energy displaces DM | FADING | Cluster lensing (Brouwer et al. 2017 supports; Ettori et al. 2022 disfavours); no covariant theory; no CMB fit |
| **Mirror DM / Twin Higgs** | Foot 2004+; Chacko-Goh-Harnik 2006 | Dark sector is mirror copy of SM; discrete Z₂ symmetry; DM = mirror baryons | ACTIVE | Twin Higgs solves hierarchy; mirror DM direct detection signatures; BBN constraint on dark radiation |
| **Self-interacting DM (SIDM)** | Spergel-Steinhardt 2000 | σ/m ∼ 1 cm²/g; solves core-cusp, too-big-to-fail | ACTIVE | Cluster offsets (Bullet, Abell 520) set σ/m < 1 cm²/g at cluster scale; velocity-dependent models viable |
| **Early Dark Energy (EDE)** | Poulin-Smith-Karwal-Kamionkowski 2019 | Axion-like field releases energy near matter-radiation equality; boosts H₀ | ACTIVE | ACT DR6 + DESI DR2: f_EDE = 0.09 ± 0.03, H₀ ≈ 71 km/s/Mpc; residual SH0ES tension ∼2σ (Poulin-Smith 2026) |
| **Dark Dimension + KK graviton DM** | Montero-Vafa-Valenzuela 2022; Anchordoqui-Antoniadis-Lüst 2023; Gonzalo et al. 2023 | Species-scale Swampland: one mesoscopic extra dim ℓ ∈ [0.1, 10] μm; meV KK gravitons = DM | ACTIVE | c' = 1/6 from Swampland; N_eff = 2.86 ± 0.13 (ACT DR6 Calabrese 2025) compatible; Eöt-Wash ISL next-gen test |
| **NMC quintessence** | Faraoni 2004; Ye 2025; Wolf et al. 2025; Pan-Ye 2026 | Non-minimally coupled thawing scalar ξ_χ R χ²/2; dynamical DE with PPN-Cassini constraint | ACTIVE | DESI DR2 3.3σ from phantom-crossing region (joint property of thawing family, not ECI-specific); DR3/LSST Y10 discriminant |
| **Interacting Dark Energy (IDE)** | Wetterich 1995+; many | DM-DE coupling Q; alters evolution of both; can address H₀ | ACTIVE | DESI DR2 IDE fits; tension with CMB if Q too large; no unique signal |
| **Phantom / quintom** | Caldwell 2002 (phantom); Feng et al. 2005 (quintom) | w < −1 scalar (phantom); or field crossing w = −1 (quintom) | ACTIVE (preferred by data) | DESI DR2 + DESY5 central values prefer phantom-crossing region; quintom models populate this; no ghost-free single-field phantom without higher-derivative operators |
| **Cold-thawing quintessence** | Steinhardt-Wang-Zlatev 1999; Scherrer-Sen 2008 | Scalar frozen at early times, begins rolling; w approaches −1; tracker attractors | FADING (vs data) | Entire thawing family at 3.3σ from DESI DR2+DESY5 mean; cannot reach phantom-crossing region |
| **Emergent dark energy** | Li-Shafieloo 2019; Lambert et al. 2020 ⚠ | DE equation of state emerges from a transition at late time; phenomenological | ACTIVE (niche) | Fits DESI DR2 as well as wCDM; no microscopic model |

## 5. Inflation Models

| Model | Proposer + year | Core claim | Status 2026 | Decisive event |
|-------|----------------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Chaotic inflation** | Linde 1983 | Monomial potentials V ∝ φ^n; large field inflation; high r | REFUTED (original) | Planck 2015/2018: φ² → r ∼ 0.13 excluded at >2σ; φ⁴ excluded; monomial family ruled out |
| **Natural inflation** | Freese-Frieman-Olinto 1990 | Pseudo-Goldstone boson; cosine potential; f ≳ M_P required | FADING | Planck+BICEP/Keck: f > M_P needed; sub-Planckian f excluded; survives at very large f only |
| **α-attractors** | Kallosh-Linde 2013+ | Hyperbolic field space; universal attractor predictions n_s = 1 − 2/N, r = 12α/N² | ACTIVE (favoured) | Excellent Planck fit for α = O(1); BICEP/Keck r < 0.036 consistent; LiteBIRD will test |
| **Starobinsky R² / plateau** | Starobinsky 1980; revived Mukhanov-Chibisov 1981 | R² term in gravity → effectively slow-roll inflation with plateau potential | ACTIVE (favoured) | Planck best-fit: n_s ≈ 0.965, r ≈ 0.004; BICEP/Keck consistent; LiteBIRD definitive test |
| **Axion monodromy** | Silverstein-Westphal 2008 | Wrapped brane in flux compactification; repeated monodromy generates large-field inflation | ACTIVE | r ∼ 0.07 predicted (model-dependent); within LiteBIRD reach; Swampland distance conjecture tension |
| **Warm inflation** | Berera 1995+ | Inflaton dissipates into radiation during inflation; no separate reheating; modified power spectrum | ACTIVE (niche) | Modified consistency relations; different r − n_s trajectory; CMB-S4 / LiteBIRD potential discriminant |
| **Hybrid inflation** | Linde 1994 | Two-field inflation; waterfall field ends inflation; small r | FADING | n_s predictions model-dependent; not generically favoured; survives in BSM embeddings |
