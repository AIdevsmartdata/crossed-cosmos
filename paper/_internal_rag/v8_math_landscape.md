## Consolidated verdict: **TRIANGULATED NEGATIVE**

Three independent surveys, on three distinct mathematical programmes,
converge on the same conclusion:

**No HOOK-RIGOROUS connection exists today between any of the four v6
ingredients (type-II crossed-product algebra $\AR$, modular flow
$\sigma^R_{\tauR}$, $k$-design complexity $\Ck$, PH activator
$\Theta(\mathrm{PH}_k)$) and any of: condensed mathematics, cohesive
∞-topoi, p-adic / adelic / motivic physics, Bost–Connes arithmetic
thermodynamics, Connes–Consani–Moscovici prolate spectral realisation.**

Best available connections are HOOK-PROGRAMMATIC (research-programme
goals, no theorem):

1. **Sati–Schreiber Hypothesis H** claims flux-quantised observables as
   a programme goal, hitting v6 ingredient (i) type-II and (iii)
   complexity *as declared targets*, not as proven theorems
   (Sati–Schreiber 2002.11093, 2411.00628, 2501.17927).

2. **Bost–Connes (1995) + Consani–Moscovici 2024–2025** (arXiv
   2310.18423, 2401.08401, 2511.22755) provide a **rigorous p-adic
   Tomita–Takesaki theory** — on **type III₁** factors at physical $\beta$.
   Three structural mismatches with v6 $\AR$:
   - factor type (III₁ vs II$_\infty$),
   - time interpretation (arithmetic $\log n$ scaling vs QRF /
     Connes–Rovelli thermal on a gravitational subregion),
   - phase-transition content ($\beta{=}1$ Galois symmetry breaking
     vs GSL monotonicity).
   Takesaki-dualising BC to a type-II$_\infty$ core is routine but does
   not identify the resulting time with QRF time. This remains open
   research, not a substitution.

3. **Kashiwara–Schapira microlocal sheaves** (not condensed/cohesive)
   provide a rigorous home for persistent homology *as a derived-category
   object*. This is HOOK-PROGRAMMATIC for (iv): a categorical setting
   that could replace the classical scalar-field formulation of
   $\mathrm{PH}_k[\dn]$ without new physics, purely to gain mathematical
   cleanness.

All other connections surveyed (k-design complexity over $\mathbb{Q}_p$,
motivic lift of PH, adelic modular flow on a gravitational algebra,
Langlands → complexity beyond $\mathcal{N}{=}4$ SYM) are **ANALOGY
only** — surface-level parallel, no theorem, no construction.

## What this means for v6 and v7

### v6 (current JHEP-formal paper, tag v6.0.1)

**Zero changes.** None of the surveyed programmes supplies a theorem
that would modify the inequality, the dequantisation map, or the proof
sketch. The v6 outlook §7 already mentions SymTFT + Langlands as
far-future structural extensions (commit `230e728`); no addition is
warranted without a theorem in hand.

### v7 (hypothetical cohesive–SymTFT companion, not started)

**Do not start.** The mathematical ingredients are not in place. Any
such companion paper would be either (a) programmatic speculation, or
(b) a new theorem that has to be written *first* in a dedicated maths
paper. Writing a physics paper whose central claim rests on unproven
mathematical conjectures is exactly the failure mode our v6 adversarial
pipeline is designed to catch (PRINCIPLES rules 1, 12, 16, V6-1).

### What we CAN write honestly

A **5-line research memo** (this file is it) noting that the three
programmes are **the three to watch** for future development. If any
of the following three events materialises by 2028, re-evaluate:

- A theorem identifying a Connes-style crossed-product decomposition of
  a Bost–Connes dual system with a type-II$_\infty$ arithmetic core
  admitting a Connes–Rovelli thermal-time interpretation.
- A Sati–Schreiber publication establishing, rigorously, cohesive
  observables on a fibre bundle whose modular automorphism realises
  the CLPW crossed product.
- A Consani–Connes or Marcolli paper extending the prolate spectral
  realisation to a genuine type-II semifinite trace with modular flow
  generated by $\log p$ on a *non-arithmetic* (i.e. cosmological) QRF.

Until then: v6.1 as-shipped remains the defensible draft.
