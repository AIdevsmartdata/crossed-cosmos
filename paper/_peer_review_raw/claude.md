Q1. The single weakest claim is A3 (Cryptographic Censorship) as invoked in Section 5 to license the "Big Bang as decodability boundary" and the algebraic necessity of inflation. The author correctly flags A3 as a working conjecture proven only in AdS/CFT, but the paper nevertheless deploys it to make cosmological statements (past computational horizon, inflaton-as-clock-QRF, N~60 from entropy-bound saturation) without providing even a heuristic dictionary from k-design pseudorandomness to FLRW horizon physics. As a result, the most ambitious narrative claims in the paper rest on an un-transposed theorem. The fact that Section 5 contains no equations while being sold as explanatory power is the tell.

Q2. The single strongest claim is the closed-form NMC extension of the Scherrer-Sen thawing relation in Eq. (14), with the explicit coefficient B(Omega_Lambda) = (8/sqrt(3)) A(Omega_Lambda) and its quantitative use in Section 3.5 to show that the ECI NMC band half-width is ~4% of sigma_wa at DR2+DESY5 precision. This is an honest, derivable, checkable statement: it yields a specific prediction (Prediction 1b), it identifies the precise observational horizon (DR3 / LSST Y10) where the prediction becomes discriminative, and it plainly admits that at DR2 precision the model does not separate from minimal-coupling wCDM. The Cassini PPN re-derivation feeding |xi_chi| <~ 2.4e-2 is internally consistent and dimensionally clean.

Q3. Major revisions. The paper is a creditable framework-genre synthesis and the Section 3.5 / 3.6 additions are genuine technical content (PPN bound, NMC-thawing coefficient, Swampland cross-constraint with explicit resolution tree), which clears the EPJ C framework-section bar in principle. However, three items block acceptance as-is: (i) the 3.3 sigma tension with DR2+DESY5 is acknowledged but not quantitatively defended - the paper needs at minimum a joint (xi_chi, f_EDE) likelihood slice showing the EDE sector relieves the phantom-crossing pressure, or an explicit concession that ECI is non-competitive on current data; (ii) Section 5 ("Primordial cosmology") deploys A3 far beyond its flagged conjectural status and must be either demoted to a Discussion subsection or supported by at least a toy calculation; (iii) the A4/A5 Swampland cross-constraint concludes with three mutually exclusive "resolutions" without committing to one, leaving the framework's predictive content under-specified. None of these are fatal; all are addressable in one revision cycle.

Q4. Compute the joint chi-squared surface on (xi_chi, f_EDE, z_c) against DR2 BAO + Pantheon+ + Planck TT/TE/EE at fixed A(Omega_Lambda) using a minimal background-only Boltzmann integration (no full MCMC), tabulated on a 20x20x10 grid, and report the Delta chi^2 relative to LCDM and to minimal-coupling thawing. This would upgrade Prediction 1b from a geometric band-width statement to an actual goodness-of-fit claim and convert the current Mahalanobis handwave into a defensible likelihood argument.
