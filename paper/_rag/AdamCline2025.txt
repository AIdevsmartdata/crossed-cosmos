Title: Comparing Minimal and Non-Minimal Quintessence Models to 2025 DESI Data
Authors: Husam Adam, Mark P. Hertzberg, Daniel Jiménez-Aguilar, Iman Khan
Submitted: 2025-09-16; revised 2025-10-09
Journal: JCAP 04 (2026) 052
DOI: 10.1088/1475-7516/2026/04/052

NOTE: External audit listed "Adam, Cline et al." — the verified author list is Adam, Hertzberg, Jiménez-Aguilar, Khan. Bibkey kept as AdamCline2025 per audit-specified key.

ABSTRACT (summary): Tests minimal and non-minimal quintessence potentials against 2025 DESI data suggesting evolving dark-energy equation of state. Finds only modest improvements over LCDM. Non-minimally coupled scalars can transiently achieve w<-1 but for light scalars induce fifth-force and varying G_N signatures tightly constrained by tests of gravity.

RELEVANCE: Constrains NMC parameter space. Our D15 chameleon slope alpha = 0.095 lies within the gravity-test-consistent (screened) window.
