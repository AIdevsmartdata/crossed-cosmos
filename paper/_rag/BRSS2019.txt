Barbón, J.L.F.; Rabinovici, E.; Shir, R.; Sinha, R. — "On the Evolution of Operator Complexity Beyond Scrambling"
arXiv:1907.05393 [hep-th] (2019)
JHEP 10 (2019) 264. DOI: 10.1007/JHEP10(2019)264

Abstract: K-complexity of ETH operators exhibits behavior consistent with bulk evolution of extremal volumes/actions. After rapid exponential growth during scrambling, K-complexity shows linear growth for exponentially long times before saturating at an entropy-dependent constant. Discusses K-entropy with S_K ∼ ln C_K relationship.

Why cited (v6): relation S_K ∼ ln C_K between Krylov entropy and complexity.
