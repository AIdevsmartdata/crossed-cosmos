Bianconi, Ginestra — "Gravity from entropy"
arXiv:2408.14391 [gr-qc] (2024)
Phys. Rev. D 111, 066001 (2025). DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.111.066001

Abstract: Derives gravity from an entropic action coupling matter to geometry. Spacetime metric treated as a renormalizable effective density matrix; matter fields described topologically via Dirac–Kähler formalism. An alternative metric induced by matter fields enters as quantum relative entropy between the two metrics. Yields modified Einstein equations reducing to standard Einstein (zero Λ) at low coupling; a G-field Lagrange multiplier produces a dressed Einstein–Hilbert action with emergent small positive Λ.
