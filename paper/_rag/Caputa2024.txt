Caputa, Pawel; Magan, Javier M.; Patramanis, Dimitrios; Tonni, Erik — "Krylov complexity of modular Hamiltonian evolution"
arXiv:2306.14732 [hep-th] (submitted 2023-06-26)
Phys. Rev. D 109, 086004 (2024). DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.109.086004

Abstract: Complexity of states/operators evolved with the modular Hamiltonian in the Krylov basis, for QM, 2d CFT, and random modular Hamiltonians. Spread complexity is universally governed by the modular Lyapunov exponent λ^mod_L = 2π at late times. Entanglement spectrum offers insights beyond entanglement entropy.

Why cited (v6): modular flow × Krylov complexity; anchors the modular-flow Lyapunov 2π used in κ_R ≡ 2π T_R.
