Carrasco, Rafael; Pedraza, Juan F.; Svesko, Andrew; Weller-Davies, Zachary — "Gravitation from optimized computation: Einstein and beyond"
arXiv:2306.08503 [hep-th] (2023; rev 2023-09-28)
JHEP 09 (2023) 167. DOI: 10.1007/JHEP09(2023)167

Abstract: Gravitational physics arises from spacetime optimizing the computational cost of its quantum dynamics. Extends prior linearized/holographic result: derives higher-derivative gravity via corrections to C=V, and semi-classical equations via leading quantum corrections, in 2d dilaton gravities where backreaction is exactly solvable.
