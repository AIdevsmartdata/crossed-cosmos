Connes, Alain; Rovelli, Carlo — "Von Neumann Algebra Automorphisms and Time-Thermodynamics Relation in Generally Covariant Quantum Theories"
arXiv:gr-qc/9406019 (1994)
Class. Quantum Grav. 11, 2899 (1994). DOI: 10.1088/0264-9381/11/12/007

Abstract: Proposes the thermal time hypothesis: in a generally covariant quantum theory, the physical time flow is determined by the thermodynamical state of the system. Implemented via the Tomita–Takesaki theorem, which derives a one-parameter automorphism of a von Neumann algebra (modular flow) from any faithful state.

Why cited (v6): origin of modular time τ_R; foundational for κ_R ≡ 2π T_R identification.
