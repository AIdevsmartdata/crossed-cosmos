Eling, Christopher; Guedens, Raf; Jacobson, Ted — "Non-equilibrium Thermodynamics of Spacetime"
arXiv:gr-qc/0602001 (submitted 2006-02-01)
Phys. Rev. Lett. 96, 121301 (2006). DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.96.121301

Abstract: Einstein's equations emerge from requiring the Clausius relation to hold for all local acceleration horizons. With curvature corrections polynomial in the Ricci scalar, a non-equilibrium approach becomes necessary: field equations follow from an entropy balance relation with bulk viscosity entropy production term determined by energy-momentum conservation. Shows entropy production can also be accommodated within standard Einstein theory via horizon shear viscosity.

Why cited (v6): closest formal precursor dS = δQ/T + d_i S for §3 equality.
