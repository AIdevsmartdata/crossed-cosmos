Fan, Zhong-Ying — "The growth of operator entropy in operator growth"
arXiv:2206.00855 [hep-th] (2022)
JHEP 08 (2022) 232. DOI: 10.1007/JHEP08(2022)232

Abstract: Examines limits on how operator entropy S_K expands during operator growth. Establishes a dispersion bound |∂_t S_K| ≤ 2 b_1 ΔS_K via uncertainty, where b_1 is the first Lanczos coefficient. For irreversible processes this is not tight at late times; a stronger asymptotic constraint follows from a universal logarithmic relation between Krylov complexity and operator entropy, describing long-time behavior in chaotic and integrable systems.

Why cited (v6): tightest Krylov precursor to dS_gen/dτ_R source law; we demarcate our k-design source from this logarithmic form.
