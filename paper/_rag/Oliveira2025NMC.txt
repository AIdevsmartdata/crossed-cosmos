arXiv:2510.08459
Title: The dark sector of the Universe as a scalar field in Horndeski Gravity
Authors: M. S. Oliveira, F. A. Brito, J. A. V. Campos
Submitted: 2025-10-09; revised 2026-03-16
Accepted: JCAP

ABSTRACT (verbatim):
In the present work, we study a subclass of Horndeski gravity characterized by a non-minimal derivative coupling between a scalar field and the Einstein tensor, as a possible alternative to alleviate the observational tension associated with estimates of the Hubble constant H_0.

RESULT: Model fits late-time cosmic expansion and yields H_0 values more compatible with local (SH0ES) measurements. Directly relevant to §3.2 (Hubble tension) as empirical support for non-minimal coupling mechanism underlying xi_chi.
