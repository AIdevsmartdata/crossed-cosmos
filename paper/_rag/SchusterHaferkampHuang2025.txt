Schuster, Thomas; Haferkamp, Jonas; Huang, Hsin-Yuan — "Random unitaries in extremely low depth"
arXiv:2407.07754 [quant-ph] (2024; rev 2025-01-04)
Science (2025).

Abstract: Random quantum circuits generate approximate unitary k-designs on n qubits in log-depth; pseudorandom unitaries (PRU) in 1D circuits with polylog depth. Shallow circuits have low complexity and short-range entanglement yet are indistinguishable from exponentially complex unitaries. Applications to classical shadows and quantum advantage.

Why cited (v6): short-depth PRU existence underpinning the C_k k-design-complexity source term.
