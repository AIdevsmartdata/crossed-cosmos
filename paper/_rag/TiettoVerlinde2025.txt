Title: A microscopic model of de Sitter spacetime with an observer
Authors: Damiano Tietto, Herman Verlinde
Submitted: 2025-02-06
DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.2502.03869

ABSTRACT (summary): Simple microscopic quantum mechanical model of low-dimensional dS holography with an observer. Using semiclassical gravity, derives formula for total entropy of 3D Schwarzschild-de Sitter universe with observer; matches spectral density of double-scaled SYK (DSSYK). Explains appearance of two notions of temperature in DSSYK.

RELEVANCE: Independent microscopic confirmation of observer-dependent dS entropy formula consistent with CLPW crossed-product Type II algebra. Reinforces A1 axiom.
