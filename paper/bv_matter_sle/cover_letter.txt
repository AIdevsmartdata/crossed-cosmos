Dear Editor,

I would like to submit the attached paper as a sequel to Banerjee-Niedermaier 2023 (arXiv:2305.11388) extending the SLE Hadamard construction from Bianchi I to Bianchi V matter cosmologies. The result is UNCONDITIONAL (no Hadamard hypothesis required) thanks to the H³ spectral gap.

**Subject and contribution.** The paper proves: on Bianchi V matter cosmology with conformally-coupled massless real scalar field, the SLE state (Sorkin-Johnston-style minimum of the smearing functional) is Hadamard. The proof uses Kontorovich-Lebedev decomposition on H³ with eigenfunction φ_ρ(χ) = sin(ρχ)/(ρ sinh χ) of -Δ_{H³} with eigenvalue ρ²+1, and the H³ spectral gap inf σ(-Δ_{H³}) = 1 acts as IR regulator killing the volume-zero-mode log² divergence that universally blocks massless Hadamard on Bianchi I/Heisenberg/flat-FRW.

**Why this matters.** Bianchi V matter is THE ONLY Type-A Bianchi case where the conformally-coupled massless scalar is unconditionally Hadamard. The other 8 Bianchi types either fail (BI/BIX past-Big-Bang non-Hadamard via S1+S3 obstruction; massless universal log IR) or are Hadamard-conditional (BII Heisenberg, BVI₀ Sol, BVII₀ Bieberbach, BVIII Mixmaster).

**Critical correction caught.** A previous draft contained the equation S'' + (ρ²+1)S = 0 for the Liouville-transformed mode. Sympy verification reveals the correct equation is u_ρ'' + [ρ² + 1 - 2/τ²] u_ρ = 0 with V_eff(τ) = a''/a = 2/τ² ≠ 0 for dust. The Hadamard argument is unaffected — the spectral gap +1 absorbs V_eff(τ) ≤ 2/τ_0² for τ_0 > √2 — but the corrected equation is now in the paper.
