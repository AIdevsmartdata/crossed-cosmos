Subject: Submission of paper: States of Low Energy on Bianchi VI₀ massive scalar
         Sol cosmologies — full Hadamard property (Wave-6 update)

Dear Editor,

This paper extends Banerjee–Niedermaier 2023 (Bianchi I) and Them–Brum 2013
(homogeneous and inhomogeneous spacetimes with compact Cauchy surfaces) to the
Bianchi VI₀ massive scalar SLE on Sol-solvmanifold spatial slices.  Three
lemmas are now closed (massive case):

  Lemma C — Uniform mode-frequency positivity.
            ω²_{n,λ}(t) ≥ m² + R_min/6 > 0, UNCONDITIONAL via the exact
            Mathieu lower bound V_min = 2 λ² √(αβ).
            NOTE: this V_min is QUADRATIC in λ, NOT linear (Wave-3 correction).

  Lemma D — Adiabatic order ≥ 2.
            Substantially closed via DLMF §10.40 Debye expansion of K_{iν} and
            standard Parker / BN23 adiabatic counting.
            |β_{n,λ}|² = O(λ⁻⁴), better in the L²-Bogolubov norm.
            One technical gap remains (n-uniformity), 1–2 weeks of work.

  Lemma E — Sol-Plancherel wavefront-set preservation (Wave-6, this letter).
            WF(W₂) ⊆ N⁺ × N⁻ via Hörmander Vol. I Thms. 8.1.9 + 8.2.13
            (oscillatory integral + microlocal dominated convergence) applied
            sector-by-sector, plus propagation of singularities (Radzikowski
            1996 Thm. 3.1) and the Them–Brum 2013 microlocal Sobolev
            framework.  No new microlocal machinery required: the proof is
            essentially "transfer of standard calculus + one explicit
            invariance check (Sol boost preserves the null cone)".

Hard negative for the massless case.
            Universal log IR divergence as λ → 0 (same obstruction as
            BN23 §4 Bianchi I and Olbermann 2007 §4.2 FLRW).  We state this
            as Obstruction A; only m > 0 saves the construction.

Time-to-publication, massive case: 1–2 months (down from 9–12 months
            originally and 3–5 months after closing C, D).  Remaining work
            is Gap 1 (n-uniformity, technical) and Obstruction F
            (Γ\Sol quotient bookkeeping).

Hallucination corrections caught in the chain (cumulative):
   - ξ = 1/8 → 1/6 (3+1 D, not 2+1).
   - V_min = 2 λ² √(αβ) (quadratic), corrected from earlier "linear in λ".
   - "Brum–Them" citation order corrected to Them–Brum (verified arXiv:1302.3174).

Kévin Remondière
