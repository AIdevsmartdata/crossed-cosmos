Subject: Submission of paper: States of Low Energy on Bianchi VII_0 cosmologies with Bieberbach quotient spatial slices

Dear Editor,

This paper extends Banerjee-Niedermaier 2023 (J. Math. Phys. 64:113503,
arXiv:2305.11388) to Bianchi VII_0 cosmologies with compact flat
Bieberbach 3-manifold spatial slices.

**Result.** We construct a States-of-Low-Energy (SLE) quasifree Hadamard
state for the conformally coupled massless scalar field on every
Bianchi VII_0 cosmology whose spatial Cauchy slice is one of the five
orientable cyclic-holonomy Bieberbach 3-manifolds (G_1, G_2, G_3, G_4, G_5
in the Wolf 1972 / Charlap 1986 numbering).  The proof is now complete
and unconditional; no residual gaps.

**Bieberbach compatibility (Lemma 7).** Of the 10 affine equivalence
classes of compact flat 3-manifolds, exactly G_1-G_5 (orientable cyclic
holonomy in {e}, Z_2, Z_3, Z_4, Z_6) are realised as VII_0 quotients.
The Hantzsche-Wendt G_6 (Z_2 x Z_2, non-cyclic, no order-4 element) is
NOT a VII_0 quotient.  G_7-G_10 are non-orientable, holonomy contains
reflections, excluded.  The cyclic-vs-Z_2xZ_2 distinction and the
crystallographic restriction n in {1,2,3,4,6} are sympy-verified.

**UV anisotropic WKB (Lemma 8).** The BN23 isotropic-base Bogoliubov
bound |beta_r^(f)|^2 = O(r^{-N}) transfers verbatim to anisotropic VII_0
once the substitution a^2 -> a_1*a_2 is made in the mode frequency
omega_r^2 = r^2/(a_1*a_2) + R/6.  The rotation generator F enters only
through the radial parameter r (orbit radius in the (e_1, e_2) plane),
not as additional anisotropy.  The adiabaticity ratio q_r = O(r^{-1})
is sympy- and numerically-verified on a representative Kasner-like
background in the supplementary script.

**Parametrix matching (Lemma 9).** Both wavefront-set inclusions hold:
WF(W_2) ⊆ C^+ from Lemma 1 (Bessel J_0 evenness, kernel real-analytic)
plus Lemma 8 (UV WKB); the opposite inclusion C^+ ⊆ WF(W_2) follows
from W_2 - H_N being C^{N-2}-smooth (Junker-Schrohe 2002 Thm 4.4 +
BN23 UV bound), combined with the standard short-distance matching of
the leading 1/sigma coefficient at the van Vleck-Morette value
U(x,x) = 1/(4 pi^2).  This gives WF(W_2) = C^+ exactly, satisfying the
Radzikowski 1996 microlocal criterion.

**Critical closure (B3 wave).** Gap 1 (Bessel J_0 wavefront set) and
Gap 4 (IR Plancherel near r=0) closed unconditionally via the
EVENNESS of J_0: the kernel K_r(x,x') = J_0(r*rho(x,x')) is
real-analytic on R^3 x R^3 since J_0(z) has Taylor series in z^2 (only
even powers) and rho^2(x,x') is a trigonometric polynomial.  Hence
WF(K_r) = empty for fixed r > 0; the apparent square-root singularity
of rho at the diagonal is illusory.
