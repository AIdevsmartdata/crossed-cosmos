This paper extends Banerjee-Niedermaier 2023 (J. Math. Phys. 64:113503, arXiv:2305.11388) to Bianchi VII₀ cosmologies with compact flat Bieberbach 3-manifold spatial slices.

**Result.** The SLE state for the conformally-coupled massless scalar on Bianchi VII₀ × Bieberbach quotient is Hadamard, modulo a UV anisotropic WKB closure (3-6 weeks).

**Bieberbach compatibility.** Of the 10 affine equivalence classes of flat 3-manifolds, only G₁-G₅ (cyclic SO(2) holonomy) are compatible with the VII₀ rotation generator structure. Hantzsche-Wendt G₆ (Z₂×Z₂ holonomy) is NOT a VII₀ quotient; G₇-G₁₀ non-orientable excluded.

**Critical closure (B3).** Gap 1 (Bessel J₀ wavefront set) and Gap 4 (IR Plancherel near r=0) closed UNCONDITIONALLY in this revision via the EVENNESS of J₀: the kernel K_r(x,x') = J₀(r·ρ(x,x')) is real-analytic on R³ × R³ since J₀(z) has Taylor series in z² (only even powers) and ρ²(x,x') is a trigonometric polynomial. Hence WF(K_r) = ∅ for fixed r > 0; the apparent square-root singularity of ρ at the diagonal is illusory.
