Dear Editor,

I would like to submit the attached paper for consideration in Communications in Mathematical Physics or Annales Henri Poincaré.

**Subject and contribution.** The paper derives an explicit closed-form expression for the modular Hamiltonian K_FRW associated to the comoving causal diamond D_R(η_c, x_c) of a spatially-flat Friedmann-Robertson-Walker spacetime, for the conformally-coupled massless real scalar field in the conformal vacuum:

  K_FRW = (π/R) ∫_{D_R} d³x (R² - (η-η_c)² - |x-x_c|²) a(η_c)² T^FRW_00 + c_0

obtained as the conformal pullback of the Hislop-Longo (1982) Minkowski-diamond modular Hamiltonian under the unitary intertwiner U: H_Mink → H_FRW supplied by Frob (2023, JHEP 12:074).

**Two load-bearing technical lemmas:**

(1) Lemma 3.3 (stress-tensor conjugation): U⁻¹ T^Mink_00 U = a(η)^(+2) · 𝒯^FRW_00, sympy-verified off-shell on three test FRW backgrounds. (3 PASS assertions in companion sympy_lemma33.py.)

(2) Lemma 3.4 (conformal-anomaly bookkeeping): explicit closed-form coefficients

  Δ_anom[D_R] = +R⁴/(1800 η_c⁴)  (radiation era, a(η)=η)
  Δ_anom[D_R] = -R⁴/(90 η_c⁴)   (matter era, a(η)=η²)

with uniform η_c⁻⁴ scaling and matter sign negative driven by □R = -216/η¹². 28 sympy assertions PASS plus 2 mpmath@200dps cross-checks.

**Independent expert review.** The Lemma 3.3 stress-tensor conjugation has been independently verified by an external symbolic computation. The Lemma 3.4 anomaly form has been cross-checked against Mistral large-latest analytic reasoning (matched on Birrell-Davies vs (a,c)-anomaly identity □R/(2880π²) regulator ambiguity).

**Type II_∞ structure.** The construction couples to Witten 2022 (arXiv:2112.12828) type II_∞ crossed-product algebra of the comoving observer. The anomaly contribution Δ_anom is c-number-valued, hence central in N(D_R), preserving factoriality.

**Open question.** The boundary ∂D_R is not a Killing horizon of g_FRW. The leading shape-dependent residual from the extrinsic curvature of ∂D_R in the FRW bulk is O(RH(η_c)) = O(R/η_c), sub-leading for R ≪ η_c. Full computation deferred to a companion note (Open Question 3.5).

**Length and references.** ~5 pages of LaTeX; ~14 references (12 arXiv-API verified, 2 pre-arXiv DOI-verified including Hislop-Longo CMP 84:71-85 (1982) DOI 10.1007/BF01208372 and Bunch-Davies 1977 PRSA A 356:569 DOI 10.1098/rspa.1977.0151).
