I am submitting "Universal Analog-Hawking Saturation Ratio rho = c/12 for
Unitary Diagonal Minimal-Invariant-Partition CFTs" for consideration in
Letters in Mathematical Physics.

The main result is a five-line proof that the analog-Hawking saturation ratio
rho = (2pi)^{-2} int_0^inf S_BW(u) du equals c/12 for all unitary 2D CFTs
with a diagonal (A-series) modular-invariant partition function, where S_BW(u)

as a polariton falsifier pending experimental precision.

I note that the polariton prediction is a preliminary proposal; direct
experimental comparison requires further analysis of the phonon spectrum, and
