Title: Two LMFDB identifications for hatted weight-5 multiplets of the metaplectic cover S'_4

Authors: Kévin Remondière (Independent researcher, Tarbes, France)

Abstract:
We identify two LMFDB newforms that realise the hatted weight-5 multiplets of the
metaplectic double cover S'_4 at conductor 4 with nebentypus chi_4. The identification
proceeds via an explicit Hecke-eigenvalue comparison against the LMFDB database and
exploits the Cohen-Oesterlé dimension formula dim S_5^new(Gamma_0(4),chi_4) = 1 to
establish uniqueness. The result provides the number-theoretic foundation for the ECI
modular Yukawa programme and yields a structural fingerprint: the ratio of Hecke
eigenvalues T(p) for the two newforms equals the modular ratio predicted by the
Littlest Modular Seesaw at tau=i.
