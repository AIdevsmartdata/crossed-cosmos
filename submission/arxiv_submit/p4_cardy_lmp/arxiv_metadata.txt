Title: Universal Analog-Hawking Saturation Ratio rho = c/12
       for Unitary Diagonal Minimal-Invariant-Partition CFTs

Authors: Kévin Remondière (Independent researcher, Tarbes, France)

Abstract: (use verbatim abstract from cardy_rho_paper.tex)
We prove that the analog-Hawking saturation ratio rho = (2pi)^{-2} int_0^inf S_BW(u) du
equals c/12 for every unitary 2D CFT with diagonal (A-series) modular-invariant partition
function and central charge c > 0. The proof reduces to the Carlitz identity. Numerical
verification for four CFTs to 4-digit precision: free boson (c=1, rho=1/12), free Majorana
fermion (c=1/2, rho=1/24), Tricritical Ising M(4,5) (c=7/10, rho=7/120), 3-state Potts
M(5,6) (c=4/5, rho=1/15). Extension to parastatistics: rho_{p,k} = k/[12(k+1)]. The
prediction rho=1/18 for order-k=2 anyons is proposed as a polariton falsifier. Full
verification against Cardy entropy formula and Bisognano-Wichmann thermal state included.
