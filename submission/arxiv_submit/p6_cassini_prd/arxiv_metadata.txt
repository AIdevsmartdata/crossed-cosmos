Title: ECI Cassini-clean scalar field: post-Newtonian analytic reference,
       Palatini sub-branch, and first real-data posterior on
       DESI DR2 + Pantheon+ + Planck 2018

Authors: K. Remondière (Independent researcher, Tarbes, France)

Abstract: (use verbatim abstract from cassini_palatini_prd.tex)
The ECI research programme employs a non-minimally coupled scalar field chi with
Jordan-frame coupling L ⊃ -1/2 xi_chi R chi^2 and enforces a Cassini compliance wall
|xi_chi|(chi_0/M_P)^2 < 6e-6 from the Cassini Doppler bound |gamma-1| <= 2.3e-5.
We graft the unified post-Newtonian treatment of Karam, Sanchez Lopez, and Terente Diaz
(KSTD 2026, arXiv:2604.16226), providing closed-form expressions for G_eff(r) and PPN
parameter gamma(r) in both metric and Palatini formalisms. The ECI Cassini wall follows
analytically from KSTD eq. (3.42). We register a Palatini sub-branch (ECI v7.5-P) as a
conditional 2027-decision direction. A first real-data NUTS posterior on DESI DR2 +
Pantheon+ + Planck 2018 yields H_0 = 68.51 +/- 0.14 km/s/Mpc and xi_chi rail-saturated
at -0.024, consistent with 1-loop RG running from xi(M_Z)~+0.001 to xi(M_GUT)~-0.029.
Decisive falsifier: BepiColombo Mercury 2027 PPN improvement to |gamma-1| < 1e-6.
